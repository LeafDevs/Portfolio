About Me

Greetings! I'm a 17-year-old tech enthusiast with a keen interest in Robotics, Computer Science, and game development. Proficient in Java, Javascript, and Python, I specialize in both front-end and back-end web development, but strive to be better at front-end. Driven by a passion for innovation, I aspire to create immersive gaming experiences in the realm of Virtual Reality (VR) and potentially develop my own VR system. Looking ahead, I plan to pursue a Bachelor's degree in Robotics to deepen my knowledge of autonomous systems, followed by a Master's in Computer Science at the esteemed University of Amsterdam. In addition to my technical skills, I have a penchant for clean aesthetics, with white and pink as my favorite colors. White Lilies and Amaryllis are my preferred flowers, embodying the simplicity and passion that I aim to bring to my work. Join me on this exciting journey as I navigate the intersections of technology and creativity, striving to make meaningful contributions to the fields of Robotics, Computer Science, and VR game development. Together, let's shape a future where innovation and simplicity harmonize seamlessly.
